Introduction

Orenova Mining Services Co. Ltd is a specialized Sudanese company established by a team of highly experienced geologists and mining engineers with proven expertise across Africa and the Middle East region.

We provide integrated mining services, including mineral exploration, geological assessments, technical support, and sustainable development solutions for economic mineral resources.

Our goal is to deliver high-quality geological and technical services using the latest technologies and industry best practices, while providing innovative solutions to technical and operational challenges.
