Core Services

Orenova Mining Services Co. Ltd provides a comprehensive range of integrated mining services designed to support mineral exploration, evaluation, and development projects.

Geological Surveying & Mineral Exploration

Conducting detailed geological mapping, mineral exploration programs, and resource evaluation using modern techniques and methodologies.

Sampling, Assaying & Laboratory Coordination

Providing systematic sampling, sample preparation, and coordination with certified laboratories to ensure accurate analytical results.

Geophysical Surveying & Data Analysis

Performing geophysical surveys, data processing, interpretation, and modeling to identify subsurface mineralization targets.

Mining License Application & Regulatory Support

Assisting clients with mining license applications, regulatory compliance, and liaison with relevant authorities.

Technical & Economic Feasibility Studies

Preparing comprehensive technical and economic feasibility studies to support informed investment and development decisions.

Environmental Studies & Manpower Supply

Conducting environmental assessments and providing qualified manpower to support sustainable mining operations.
